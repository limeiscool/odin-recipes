Mai Tai Cocktail

Description

This Mai Tai cocktail is made with two flavors of rum mixed with pineapple juice and orange juice to make a yummy, fruity drink.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Ice Cubes
  • 3 Fluid Ounces Pineaple Juice
  • 2 Fluid Ounces Orange Juice
  • 1 (1.5 Fluid Ounce) Spiced Rum
  • 1/2 (1.5 Fluid Ounce) Coconut-Flavored Rum
  • 1 Teaspoon Grenadine Syrup

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.

  2. Fill a cocktail shaker with ice cubes.

  3. Add pineapple juice, orange juice, spiced rum, coconut rum, and grenadine. Shake vigorously and strain into a glass full of ice.

  4. Serve and enjoy!
